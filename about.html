Our vision

MindCare was created to make school mental support more accessible and more human.

We focus on practical, responsible tools that help students seek support earlier and help psychologists provide continuity of care.

Mission

Enable safer and more effective communication between students and psychologists in school communities.

Audience

Students aged 13–18 and school psychologists, with schools managing parental consent responsibilities.

Infrastructure

MindCare uses Supabase backend services with data hosted on servers in France (EU).

Trust principles

  • Privacy-focused product decisions by default
  • Clear communication for students, staff, and families
  • School-compatible governance and consent workflows
  • Secure engineering practices and ongoing quality improvements
