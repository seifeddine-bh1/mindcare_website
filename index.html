Who we are

Built for school communities where trust matters.

Our mission

mindcare helps students reach school psychologists early, safely, and respectfully. We aim to reduce friction in asking for support.

School-centered context

The app is designed for communication within the same school, supporting collaboration between students, staff, and care teams.

Our values

Privacy by design, compassionate language, practical support, and clear boundaries for student protection.

How it works

Simple steps for students and psychologists.

Student flow

  1. Sign in with your school-linked account and profile.
  2. Use Home dashboard tools (mood tracking, breathing, body scan, word game, care tasks).
  3. Send a support message or appointment request via Chats.
  4. Receive updates and reminders through notifications.

Psychologist flow

  1. Manage student communication in secure school-scoped channels.
  2. Review requests and plan appropriate follow-up.
  3. Share care tasks and monitor continuity.
  4. Use notifications to respond quickly and maintain continuity of care.

Safety and moderation overview

mindcare uses role-aware access patterns, account profiles, and protected communication flows designed for school wellbeing contexts. Schools can define escalation and moderation workflows aligned with local policy.

Privacy & safety

Data handling summary

We process account details, chat content and metadata, media uploads, notification tokens, and optional diagnostic data to deliver service, maintain safety, and provide support.

Student protection principles

We emphasize least-necessary data access, school-governed roles, secure storage, and clear deletion pathways for students and schools.
