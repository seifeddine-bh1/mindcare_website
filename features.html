Product capabilities

Everything schools need for student mental support in one platform.

🔐 Secure chat

Students and psychologists can communicate in a protected environment with anonymous or identified options.

📊 Mood tracking

Daily mood submissions with tags and optional notes help identify emotional trends early.

✅ Assigned tasks

Psychologists can assign practical tasks and track completion status to support progress over time.

📰 Guided feed

Psychologists share relevant content that educates students and normalizes mental well-being conversations.

🧘 Relief activities

Stress-relief and concentration tools are available directly in-app to improve daily self-regulation.

🏫 School-centered controls

Role-based workflows support responsible administration and clear accountability within each school.

Data types used by these features

  • Identity and account details (first name, last name, email, birthdate, school code)
  • Optional profile picture
  • Encrypted messages
  • Mood data (mood, tags, optional notes)
  • Task records and completion status
  • Basic technical logs and device information
